What's involved in taking part

This study looks at supports for families. Here's the whole journey, start to finish, so you know what to expect before you decide.

First — is this study right for you?

You'll need all three of these to take part:

Living in rural or regional Australia
Parenting at least one child aged 0–12
Raising that child with someone else (a partner, grandparent, or anyone important in your child's life)
